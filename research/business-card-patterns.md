## Scoring System

Rate each card 1-10 on:
- **Readability** (can you read it at arm's length?)
- **Professionalism** (would you trust this person in your home?)
- **Memorability** (does it stand out from others?)
- **Information** (does it have what's needed, nothing more?)

Cards scoring < 5 average = prime targets

## What GOOD Contractor Cards Look Like
- Clean, 2-color max
- Name and trade prominent
- ONE phone number (the one they answer)
- Simple logo or icon
- License number (builds trust)
- Optional: QR code to Google reviews

## Redesign Template Styles (Our Offerings)

### Style A: "Clean Professional"
- White/light background, dark text
- Bold name, subtle trade description
- Accent color from their existing brand
- Modern sans-serif font

### Style B: "Dark & Bold"
- Dark background (navy, charcoal, black)
- White/gold text
- Strong logo placement
- Premium feel

### Style C: "Trade Specific"
- Custom icon for their trade
- Earthy tones (construction) / Blue tones (plumbing) / Orange (electrical)
- Badge-style layout
- License number prominent

### Style D: "Photo Forward"
- Their work photo or portrait on card
- Clean overlay with contact info
- Most personal, best conversion
