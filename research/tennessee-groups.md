## How to Find More Groups

Search Facebook for these exact terms:
- `[city] contractors`
- `[city] handyman`
- `[city] home improvement`
- `[city] subcontractors`
- `[city] who do you recommend`
- `Tennessee [trade] network`

Cities to target: Nashville, Memphis, Knoxville, Chattanooga, Clarksville, Murfreesboro, Franklin, Johnson City, Jackson, Cookeville

## Activity Patterns

- **Monday AM**: "Happy Monday" self-promo posts (cards shared)
- **Tuesday-Thursday**: Job posting / looking for work
- **Friday**: "Weekend warrior" posts
- **Anytime**: "Does anyone know a good ___?" → responses with card photos

## Key Insight
The REFERRAL groups (Tier 3) are actually the best source — people photograph and post contractor business cards, giving us both the bad design AND proof that the contractor is actively working and getting referrals.
